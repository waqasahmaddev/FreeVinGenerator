What is a VIN Generator?

A VIN generator is a free online tool that creates random, valid Vehicle Identification Numbers (VINs) for testing, software development, and educational purposes. Each generated VIN follows the international ISO 3779 standard and includes a valid check digit to ensure structural accuracy.

Understanding VIN Number Format

A valid VIN consists of exactly 17 characters (digits and capital letters, excluding I, O, and Q to avoid confusion). The VIN structure includes:

  • Characters 1-3: World Manufacturer Identifier (WMI) - Country and manufacturer
  • Characters 4-8: Vehicle Descriptor Section (VDS) - Vehicle attributes
  • Character 9: Check digit for validation
  • Character 10: Model year
  • Character 11: Plant code
  • Characters 12-17: Sequential production number
